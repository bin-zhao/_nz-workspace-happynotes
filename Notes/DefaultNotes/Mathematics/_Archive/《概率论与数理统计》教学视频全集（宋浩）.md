运算律
	集合

交换律
	A U B = B U A
	A ∩ B = B ∩ A

结合律
	(A U B) U C = A U (B U C)
	(A ∩ B) ∩ C = A ∩ (B ∩ C)

分配律
	(A U B) ∩ C = (A ∩ C) U (B ∩ C)
	(A ∩ B) U C = (A U C) ∩ (B U C)

对偶律
	逆(A U B) = 逆(A) ∩ 逆(B)
	逆(A ∩ B) = 逆(A) U 逆(B)
